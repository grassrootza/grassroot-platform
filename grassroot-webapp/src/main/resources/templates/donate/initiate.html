In every community there are men and women who have the fire within to create change. We have built a platform to change this. It consists of purpose-built tools that solve real problems for people who wish to organize their communities. It connects leaders to each other, allows them and their communities to speak with one voice, and compel government change.

Our tools have enabled communities to build services for themselves, organize for better sanitation, tell the media about their issues, and organize for community safety. In the next year we will be scaling up our field presence and our tools. Join us as we work to create and spread the tools for a people's democracy.
